About MealMate

MealMate is your ultimate campus food companion for Trishal Campus in Mymensingh, Dhaka. We connect students with the best local dining options and homemade food from fellow students. Our platform makes it easy to discover, order, and enjoy amazing Bangladeshi food right on your campus.
