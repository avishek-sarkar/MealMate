# MealMate - Campus Food Hub

A comprehensive web platform designed to solve food-related problems on campus by connecting students with local hotels and homemade food providers.

## 🍔 Project Overview

MealMate is a hackathon project that addresses campus food challenges by providing:
- **Hotel Menu Reviews**: Daily menu updates from partner hotels with student reviews
- **Homemade Food Marketplace**: Platform for students to sell/buy homemade food
- **24-Hour Reset System**: Fresh content every day with automatic cleanup
- **Community-Driven**: Student reviews and ratings drive the platform

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. **Real-Time Newsfeed**
- Instagram-style feed showing latest reviews and food posts
- Filter options: All Posts, Menu Reviews, Homemade Food
- Time-sensitive content with countdown timers
- Like, comment, and share functionality

### 2. **Hotel Integration**
- Partner hotels can post daily menus with prices
- Students can review menu items with ratings
- Reviews automatically expire after 24 hours
- Hotel ratings based on recent reviews

### 3. **Homemade Food Marketplace**
- Students can post their homemade food for sale
- Location-based browsing (hostel blocks)
- Direct contact system between buyers and sellers
- Posts automatically expire after 24 hours

### 4. **User Authentication**
- Login required for posting reviews or selling food
- Anonymous browsing allowed for all content
- User profiles with activity tracking

### 5. **Responsive Design**
- Mobile-first approach
- Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Modern UI with smooth animations
